# By Your Grace Have Mercy - உம் கிருபையினால் என்மேல் மனமிரங்கும்

This song of deep repentance and cry for cleansing echoes Psalm 51 — David’s confession after sin with Bathsheba: “Have mercy on me, O God… Create in me a clean heart… Restore to me the joy of Your salvation.” It is raw, honest surrender — acknowledging sin, seeking purification by hyssop (symbolizing cleansing) and blood, longing for renewed spirit and joy. In the eternal day, this cleansing is complete — no more sin, no broken bones, only pure hearts in the New Jerusalem (Revelation 21:27).
Closing Prayer for the Listener
Lord Jesus Christ, who cleanses us by Your blood and creates clean hearts,
thank You for mercy that blots out transgressions and restores joy.
Purge us, renew us, let us teach transgressors Your ways.
In the eternal day, let us dwell in Zion with pure hearts forever.
Come quickly, our Purifier and Restorer.
